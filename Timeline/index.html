Timeline an app that was thrown together for the Discovery Developer Competition and it displays all of the photos from the National Archives via Flickr.

It attempts to place photos that don't always have a specific date tag onto a timeline by using some clever date parsing on the photo description, which can make some dates inaccurate!

You can scroll the view by clicking and dragging the mouse, zoom by dragging the slider or search by typing in the box. Clicking on a photo makes it bigger and displays its title and description. Clicking on a tag will show you related photos.
